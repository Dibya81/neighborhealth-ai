Ward 68 — Koramangala. Why is the dengue risk high today?
Koramangala has a high dengue risk (74/100) today. Three signals converged: 18.4mm of rainfall over the last 7 days created stagnant water near drainage channels, October's post-monsoon peak brings historically elevated case counts in this zone, and 3 community breeding spot reports were filed this week — all pointing to active vector presence.
Contributing signals
  • Rainfall 18.4mm last 7 days — 2.1× above October average
  • October post-monsoon seasonal peak — historically highest risk month
  • 3 breeding spot reports near BBMP drainage complaints
  • Temperature 24.8°C — optimal for Aedes aegypti breeding
Prevention advisory
Immediate actions
  • •Remove water from flower pots, coolers, tyres
  • •Use mosquito repellent during 6–8 AM and 6–8 PM
  • •Report stagnant water to BBMP: 1533
Watch for symptoms
High fever, severe headache, joint pain, rash. Seek medical attention within 48 hours of symptom onset — early treatment significantly improves outcomes.

Future Scope

From Bengaluru to every city.
From disease to every risk.

01 — Smart Cities
Pan-India ward intelligence
The same pipeline works for any Indian city with ward-level administrative data. Mumbai, Chennai, Delhi — identical architecture, new data sources.
02 — IoT Integration
Sensor-grade accuracy
Connect BBMP IoT drainage sensors, AQI monitors, and smart water-level sensors directly into the feature pipeline — replacing proxy signals with ground truth.
03 — Hospital Networks
Real-time case surveillance
Direct integration with hospital admission data to close the feedback loop — actual case counts per ward feeding back into model training daily.
04 — Predictive Dispatch
BBMP resource allocation
API access for municipal health workers — auto-route fogging teams, vector control, and medical camps to high-risk wards before outbreak peaks.
05 — Personal Health
Wearable + location-aware
Mobile app integration with wearable health data. Personalised risk assessment based on individual health conditions and real-time GPS location.
06 — Government API
Public health infrastructure
Open API for BBMP, KSPCB, and NHM Karnataka — turning NeighborHealth into a shared intelligence layer for all public health programs in the state.
